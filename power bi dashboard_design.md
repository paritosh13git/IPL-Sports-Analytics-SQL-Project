# Power BI Dashboard Design

## Page 1: Match Overview
- Cards: Total Matches, Cities, Teams
- Bar chart: Matches per Venue
- Bar chart: Matches per Season
- Line chart: Toss decision trends

## Page 2: Batting Analytics
- KPI: Total Runs
- Bar chart: Runs by Team
- Bar chart: Boundaries by Team
- Line chart: Year-wise runs
- Table: Top scorers (from deliveries)

## Page 3: Bowling Analytics
- KPI: Total Wickets
- Bar chart: Dot balls by Bowling Team
- Table: Top wicket types
- Table: Bowlers giving extra runs

## Page 4: Venue Analytics
- Map: Cities hosting IPL
- Bar chart: Runs by Venue
- Year slicer
